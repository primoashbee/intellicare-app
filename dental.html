Dental Care

1. Dental examination;

2. Twice (2x) per year oral prophylaxis

3. Oral health education through chairside instruction

4. Orthodontic consultation (braces & malposition of teeth)

5. Pre-natal check of teeth & gums

6. Temporo-mandibular joint consultation (clicking of jaws)

7. Emergency dental treatment for the relief of pain

8. Gum treatment for cases like inflammation or bleeding

9. Permanent lightcure fillings covered up to four (4) teeth only

10. Temporary fillings (unlimited)

11. Simple extraction (unlimited)

12. Re-cementation of fixed bridges, crowns, jackets, inlays/outlays
