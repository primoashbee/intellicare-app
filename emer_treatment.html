Treatment
/ In-Patient Care

(Emergency)

1. Involuntary Room Upgrading

  1. Covered up to seventy-two (72) hours without incremental cost (except suite room), if a member has to occupy a room one (1) level higher than what is entitled due to non-availability.
  2. After If seventy-two (72) hours, if a room becomes available or not, incremental charges will be filled to the account of the member
  3. The member is obligated to transfer to his room category if it becomes available; otherwise incremental charges shall be billed to the members from day one (1) of confinement;

2. Accredited doctor's services;

3. Medicines administered during treatment, or for immediate relief;

4. Oxygen and intravenous fluids;

5. Dressings, plaster casts, and sutures;

6. Laboratory tests, x-rays, & other diagnostic examinations directly related to the emergency management of the patient
