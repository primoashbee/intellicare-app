Reimbursement

Out Patient Care

1. Medical consultations during regular clinic hours, excluding cost of prescribed medicines;

2. Emergency room care;

3. Referral to accredited specialists;

4. Eye, Ear, Nose, Throat consultations

5. Treatment of minor injuries or illnesses (including ATS & Toxoid vaccines if indicated, except anti-rabies);

6. Laboratory tests, x-ray, and other diagnostic examination prescribed by the INTELLICARE physician;

7. Minor surgery not requiring confinement;

8. Speech & Physical therapy up to 12 sessions per year respectively;

9. Pre-natal and post-natal consultations are covered up to 14 consultations per year.
