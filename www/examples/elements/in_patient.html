In-Patient

Treatment
/In-Patient Care

1. Maximum Benefit Limit: Php110,000.00/member/illness/year

2. No deposit upon admission

3. Room & Board benefits under Semi-private category or plan Php1,000.00 whichever is higher.

4. Use of operating room & recovery room facilities;

5. Standard admission kit;

6. Professional services of all attending accredited specialists;

7. Anaesthesia & medications administered during confinement

8. Blood transfusions & intravenous fluids;

9. Laboratory tests, x-ray, & other diagnostics examinations;

10. Dressing, plaster casts, sutures & other items directly related to the medical management of the patient;

11. ICU confinement is covered up to Pre-existing Condition Limit;

12. Assistance in administrative requirements through the public relation officer;

13. All other hospital charges deemed necessary by the INTELLICARE Accredited Physician in the treatment of the patient; and,

14. Ambulance service is covered up to Php5,000.00 per conduction, (hospital to hospital & point of incident to hospital);
